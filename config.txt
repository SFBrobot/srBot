Motor Configuration

Port 1- Bottom Left Drive Motor
Port 2- Top Left Drive Motor
Port 3- Left Mobile Goal Lift Motor (Power Expander)
Port 4- Claw Motor
Port 5- Left 4-Bar Lift Motor (Power Expander)
Port 6- Right 4-Bar Lift Motor (Power Expander)
Port 7- Chain Bar Motors (Y-Cable)
Port 8- Right Mobile Goal Lift Motor (Power Expander)
Port 9- Top Right Drive Motor
Port 10- Bottom Right Drive Motor

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Power Expander Ports

3 (Left Mobile Goal Lift Motor)
5 (Left 4-Bar Lift Motor)
6 (Right 4-Bar Lift Motor)
8 (Right Mobile Goal Lift Motor)

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Sensor Configuration

Digital
Ports 1 & 2- Left Drive Encoder
Ports 3 & 4- Right Drive Encoder
Ports 5 & 6- Chain Bar Encoder
Port 7- Mobile Goal Bumper Switch
Port 8- Autonomous Color Jumper Clip
        1 = Red, 0 = Blue
Port 9- Autonomous Side Jumper Clip
        1 = Left, 0 = Right
Port 10- Autonomous Disable Jumper Clip
        1 = Disable, 0 = Enable

Analog
Port 1- 4-Bar Potentiometer
Port 2- Claw Potentiometer
